# Escalation Policy — Standard Operating Procedure

## When to Escalate

Not all issues can be resolved at the first level. Escalation is required when:

1. **Money is involved and stuck/lost**: Any transaction where the customer's money is debited but service not delivered after T+5 working days
2. **Security concerns**: Unauthorized access, fraud, suspicious activity on a retailer's account
3. **Account permanently blocked**: When standard unblocking procedures have failed
4. **Recurring issues**: Same error happening repeatedly for a retailer or in a region
5. **Compliance/Legal**: Any issue involving legal notices, regulatory queries, or compliance violations
6. **Unresolved after 72 hours**: Any support ticket not resolved within 3 business days

## Escalation Levels

### Level 1 — Self-Service
- FAQ and troubleshooting guides
- Eko Connect app help section
- AI-powered support agent
- **Target resolution time**: Immediate to 30 minutes

### Level 2 — Support Team
- Email: cs@eko.co.in
- Phone helpline (available in app)
- Handles: Transaction disputes, account issues, commission queries
- **Target resolution time**: 24 hours

### Level 3 — Specialized Teams
- **Technical Support**: Device issues, API integration, app bugs
- **Finance Operations**: Settlement disputes, commission audits, TDS queries
- **Account Management**: KYC escalations, tier upgrades, bulk onboarding
- **Security & Fraud**: Unauthorized access, suspicious transactions, fraud cases
- **Target resolution time**: 48–72 hours

### Level 4 — Management
- Escalation to Eko management for critical unresolved issues
- Customer grievance officer
- **Target resolution time**: 5–7 working days

## SLA (Service Level Agreements)

| Priority | Response Time | Resolution Time | Examples |
|----------|--------------|-----------------|----------|
| Critical | 1 hour | 4 hours | Fraud, money lost, security breach |
| High | 4 hours | 24 hours | Transaction failures, account blocked |
| Medium | 24 hours | 72 hours | Commission disputes, KYC issues |
| Low | 48 hours | 7 working days | Feature requests, general queries |

## How to Raise an Escalation

### For Retailers
1. Contact cs@eko.co.in with:
   - Your Eko retailer code
   - Registered mobile number
   - Detailed description of the issue
   - Transaction IDs (if applicable)
   - Screenshots (if applicable)
2. You will receive a ticket number for tracking
3. If not resolved within the SLA, reply to the ticket email with "ESCALATE" in the subject line

### For API Partners
1. Contact integration.support@eko.co.in with:
   - Your partner code
   - API endpoint and error details
   - Request/response logs
   - Environment (staging/production)
2. Include your IP address if it's a whitelisting issue

## Auto-Reversal Policy
Per RBI guidelines, failed financial transactions must be auto-reversed:
- AePS: Within T+5 working days
- IMPS: Within T+1 working day (usually immediate)
- NEFT: Within T+1 working day
If auto-reversal does not happen within these timelines, the retailer or customer should escalate through the channels above.
