# Account Activation & KYC — Guide

## Account Activation Process

### Step 1: Registration
1. Download the Eko Connect app
2. Enter your mobile number
3. Complete OTP verification
4. Set your Eko PIN (4-digit)
5. Your account will be created in "PENDING" status

### Step 2: KYC Submission
1. Upload your Aadhaar card (front and back)
2. Upload your PAN card
3. Take a live selfie for face verification
4. Enter your shop/business details:
   - Shop name and address
   - Type of business
   - Approximate monthly turnover
5. Submit for verification

### Step 3: Verification
- Eko's team reviews your KYC documents
- Verification typically takes 24–48 hours
- You may receive a call for address verification
- If documents are rejected, you'll receive a notification with the reason

### Step 4: Activation
- Once verified, your account status changes to "ACTIVATED"
- You can now start processing transactions
- Your initial wallet limit will be set based on your KYC tier

## Common KYC Issues

### Documents Rejected
**Common reasons**:
- Blurry or unclear document photos
- Name mismatch between Aadhaar and PAN
- Expired ID documents
- Address not matching shop location

**Solution**:
1. Re-upload clear, well-lit photos of your documents
2. Ensure name matches exactly across documents
3. Use current, valid documents only
4. If address differs, provide additional address proof (utility bill, rent agreement)

### Account Stuck in "PENDING" Status
**Causes**:
- KYC documents not yet submitted
- Documents under review
- Additional information needed

**Solution**:
1. Check your notifications in the Eko Connect app for any pending actions
2. Ensure all required documents are uploaded
3. If pending for more than 72 hours, contact support at cs@eko.co.in
4. Include your registered mobile number and Eko code in the email

### Account Temporarily Blocked
**Causes**:
- Suspicious activity detected
- Compliance check triggered
- Multiple failed authentication attempts
- Incomplete KYC renewal

**Solution**:
1. Check the Eko Connect app for any notification about the block
2. Contact cs@eko.co.in immediately with your Eko code
3. Do NOT create a new account — this may cause permanent blocking
4. Provide any requested documents or information promptly
5. Block resolution typically takes 24–72 hours

## KYC Upgrade Tiers

| Tier | Wallet Limit | Transaction Limit | Requirements |
|------|-------------|-------------------|-------------|
| Basic | ₹10,000 | ₹5,000/txn | Aadhaar + PAN |
| Standard | ₹50,000 | ₹10,000/txn | Basic + Address Proof |
| Premium | ₹2,00,000 | ₹25,000/txn | Standard + Bank Statement + Physical Verification |

To upgrade your tier:
1. Go to Settings > KYC Upgrade in Eko Connect
2. Upload the additional required documents
3. Wait for verification (usually 48–72 hours)

## Password and PIN Reset

### Forgot Eko PIN
1. Open the Eko Connect app
2. Tap "Forgot PIN" on the login screen
3. Enter your registered mobile number
4. Complete OTP verification
5. Set a new 4-digit PIN
6. Your old PIN will be immediately deactivated

### Change Password/PIN
1. Open Eko Connect > Settings > Security
2. Enter your current PIN
3. Set a new PIN
4. Confirm the new PIN
5. Never share your PIN with anyone, including Eko staff
