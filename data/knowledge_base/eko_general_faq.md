# Eko Platform — General FAQ

## What is Eko?
Eko is a Distribution-as-a-Service (DaaS) fintech platform that empowers local shopkeepers and micro-entrepreneurs across India to provide digital financial services. By partnering with Eko, retailers can offer Aadhaar banking (AePS), domestic money transfers, utility bill payments, mobile recharges, insurance, and more — all from their existing shop.

## How does Eko work?
Eko transforms small retail outlets into banking touchpoints. Retailers use the Eko Connect app or portal to process transactions for walk-in customers. The retailer earns a commission on each transaction completed. Eko handles all backend banking, compliance, and settlement operations.

## Who can become an Eko retailer?
Any individual with a valid shop or business location can register as an Eko retailer. Requirements include:
- Valid government-issued ID (Aadhaar, PAN)
- Active mobile number
- Basic smartphone with internet access
- Completed eKYC verification through the Eko app

## What services can I offer as an Eko retailer?
As an Eko partner, you can offer:
1. **Aadhaar Banking (AePS)**: Cash withdrawals and balance inquiries using Aadhaar and fingerprint
2. **Domestic Money Transfer**: Send money to any bank account in India via IMPS/NEFT
3. **Utility Bill Payments**: Electricity, gas, water, and 100+ biller payments
4. **Mobile & DTH Recharge**: Prepaid and DTH recharge for all operators
5. **Insurance**: Micro-insurance products for customers
6. **Verification Services**: PAN, Aadhaar, bank account verification

## How do I earn money with Eko?
You earn commission on every successful transaction:
- AePS cash withdrawal: ₹3–5 per transaction
- Money transfer: 0.3%–0.5% of transfer amount (₹5 minimum)
- Bill payments: ₹2–10 per payment depending on biller
- Recharges: 2%–4% of recharge amount
Commission rates may vary and are updated in the Eko Connect app.

## How do I register on Eko?
1. Download the Eko Connect app from the Play Store
2. Enter your mobile number and complete OTP verification
3. Submit your KYC documents (Aadhaar, PAN)
4. Complete biometric eKYC
5. Wait for verification (usually 24–48 hours)
6. Once approved, your account will be activated

## What is the Eko Connect app?
Eko Connect is the main app used by retailers to manage all financial services. Through Connect, you can:
- Process AePS, money transfer, bill payment, and recharge transactions
- View transaction history and commission reports
- Check your wallet balance
- Contact support

## What are the working hours?
Eko services are available 24/7 for most transaction types. However, some services depend on bank availability:
- AePS: 24/7 (subject to bank server availability)
- Money transfer (IMPS): 24/7
- Money transfer (NEFT): Bank working hours only
- Bill payments: 24/7
